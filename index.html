Detailed Steps

  • Give Medication

    Aria needs her medication every other day, or else she will start having uncontrolable watery poops.

    You'll need:
    • 1/2 a pill of cerenia
    • 1 pill pocket
    • 1 plastic hollow gel capsule

    Put 1/2 of the pill into a plastic gel capsule. Cut excess length on gel capsule (they're usually a lil too long).

    If a lot of dust from the pill gets on to the pill pocket, you might wanna rinse the pill pocket with some water.

    The pill is extremely bitter. So if she tastest the bitterness outside of the pillpocket she might not eat it.

    If you feel like her poops are very watery you can pill her 1/4 of the cerenia pill on a none pill day, and give her 1/4 of the rest of the half of the pill on pill day.

  • Scoop Litter

    This is pretty self explanatory. She will always pee in her litter box and it looks like a little puddle. When you scoop it you will notice it is deep!

    If poop is not covered in litter, you can toss it in the toilet and flush away. Otherwise:

    Toss poop in garbage bin under sink.

    PLEASE do not flush anything covered in cat litter.

    She might poop outside of her litter box tho.

    Places She Likes to Poop:
    • floor next to litter
    • in purple carrier
    • floor next to cat tree
    • other floor surface in bathroom

    I like to wipe the area she pooped in after picking it up.

    If her poop is very mushy and has no shape of real poop, might want to give her a 1/4 of a pill even if its not pill day. Then on pill day give her the other 1/4.

  • Refill Water

    Only has to be done every 3-4 days.

    Her water fountain, if you lift up the white plastic and clear plastic parts you will see an indicator near the front of min to max water levels.

    No need unplug! Use a mug and just dump more water in.

    Push the top back down. You should hear the machine be more quiet.

  • Playtime

    Please play with her using the fishing rod toy!

    DO NOT let her bite or scratch you. If she swipes at you, play with her using the fish rod. If she scratches or bites you even if it doesnt hurt, walk away. I don't want her to ever get into the habit of it. She doesnt like being pet for long periods of time so 1 or 2 pats at a time will be enough.

  • Brushtime

    If you have time and shes feeling chill and not super playful.

    She has a hair brush in one of the kitchen drawers simnikar to where her medicine was being kept.

    She likes upward brushing near her under neck!

    If she starts biting the brush a lot and swiping at it she done with that shit. You can stop.
